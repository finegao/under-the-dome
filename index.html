Climate change and it’s bad effect is now a global issue. It brings a real threat to China’s ecosystem, economic development and social development. It’s true that China has experienced an astonishing transformation in its economic status over the past 30 years, but there are costs,

Now，china is the largest emitter of green house gases. Chinese people are suffering from the toxic smog mainly caused by the burning of coal. but It’s still hard for chinese to find a balance between development and environment, because in order to eliminate the poverty and strengthen the infrastructure china has to keep the developing pace. chinese are facing a dilemma.

and further the climate change.

Choices

How china action

Facing the problem of climate change, chinese have to do a lots of choice. Someone think development shloud be priority, others hold the view that it is more important to protect the environment.Find the best way to resolve this contradition requires time, but now, China is trying to balance the relationship between them: strengthening international cooperation, investing a lot of money, developing the renewable energy, taking on reduction targets.

There is a series of environmental and climatic problems have caused Chinese vigorous argument. Resolve the problem of climate change and environment is a task without delay, but it seems that China is caught in a dilemma between development first or environment first. As time pass, we hope China will find a way out because China is not the first one and will not be the last one which face the problem of climate change.
